Rhyssa
Aquarium Companion.

Rhyssa is there to help people understand what they are seeing, move more gently when a system becomes unclear, and make steadier decisions over time.

Most aquarium questions do not have clean answers. They depend on what the tank looks like right now, what has changed recently, what species are present, what phase the system is in. Rhyssa is designed for that kind of complexity.

Thinking alongside, not diagnosing for

Rhyssa is not a diagnostic tool. She does not receive a set of parameters and return a fix. She thinks alongside the keeper — asking what the tank looks like, what has changed, what the water is doing — and helps build a reading of the situation together.

The goal is not to replace the keeper's judgement. It is to help that judgement develop. Someone who has spent time with Rhyssa tends to notice more, react less, and feel more settled in the presence of a system they do not yet fully understand.

Shaped by ARA

Rhyssa's responses are shaped by the Aquatic Rhythm Alignment framework — which means she thinks in terms of phase, tolerance, and rhythm rather than symptom and solution. She will often ask which phase the tank is in before offering anything. That question is not a delay. It is usually the beginning of an answer that is more likely to hold.
