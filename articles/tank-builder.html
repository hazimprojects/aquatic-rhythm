This is a lab, not a quiz. Add equipment from the rack, remove it, try different combinations. Each piece changes how the system behaves — and the five rhythms on the right tell you what is shifting as you build.

The five rhythms your tank lives by
WaterMovement, renewal, and stability. Shaped most directly by filtration and water changes.
BiologicalThe bacterial colony that processes waste. Lives on filter media, not in the water.
EnvironmentalTemperature, light, and physical structure. Fish orient their whole lives around these.
LivestockHow ready the system is for fish. Not just chemistry — stability and predictability too.
HumanWhat this system will ask of you. How much attention, how often, how carefully.

There are no wrong choices here. Some combinations will produce warnings — those are observations, not judgements. The lab is yours to explore.
