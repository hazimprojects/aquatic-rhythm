Community Stress Lab

Map overlapping pressures for a hypothetical freshwater mix — temperature, chemistry, space, predation risk, social tension, and dwarf shrimp safety. Built for planning and learning, not for declaring fish “compatible.”

This MVP uses simplified ranges and rules. It does not predict individual behaviour, aggression, or disease. Your real tank and your observations always come first — especially alongside the Keeper’s Log.
